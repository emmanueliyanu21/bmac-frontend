BMAC Professional Network

Professional services such as Accounting and Bookkeeping are not a priority for most entrepreneurs. It's time consuming, boring and energy-sapping. Business owners would much rather be out there doing what they do best – providing excellent goods and services. Finance is more attractive due to the immediate or urgent importance to businesses – hence the BMAC Professionals' Network.

The BMAC Professionals’ Network is a virtual network where;

  • Professionals meet businesses who need Professional Services
  • Professionals can serve more clients anywhere, anytime
  • Professionals can connect with other professionals
  • Professionals can exchange ideas with other professionals

Vision

To deliver exceptional value (Profitability, Access to finance, structure and Dynamism) for the clients by leveraging on BMAC solutions which are Accounting, Inventory Management System (IMS), HR-Payroll.

Mission

To create a virtual network of Business Professionals who provide support to businesses up and down Nigeria using the BMAC Solutions in a spirit of transparency, integrity, accuracy, innovation and collaboration.

Objectives of the Professional Network

To Professionals

Empower Accountants Pros are easily connected to Businesses Create a pool of knowledge for businesses and professionals by virtue of cataloguing enquiries and knowledge-oriented interactions Increase ability to partner with other Accountants in other regions

To SMEs around Nigeria

Assembly Line philosophy: ability for one person to service a group of businesses at a reduced cost In every local, create a pool of Professionals that Businesses in those localities can contact and hire at affordable rates - anywhere they may be in the country
